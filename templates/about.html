About

  • Mubas Resources Reservation System is the management system of various resources at the Malawi University of Business and Applied Science.

  • The Admin can add and delete the resources when required.

  • Available resources including rooms, projectors etc., are requested to issue by the users (students/lecturers) and the Staff (Receptionist) can accept or reject the booking request.

  • The resources are collected manually by the users at various locations
